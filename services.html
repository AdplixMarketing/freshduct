Our Services

Comprehensive air duct cleaning solutions for every need and budget.

Residential Duct Cleaning

Our residential service thoroughly removes dust, debris, pet dander, pollen, and other contaminants from your home's entire HVAC duct system. We use powerful HEPA-filtered negative air machines and rotary brush systems to ensure every inch of your ductwork is clean.

  • Complete supply and return duct cleaning
  • Register and grille cleaning
  • HEPA-filtered vacuum technology
  • Before-and-after photo documentation
  • Includes all accessible ductwork

Commercial Duct Cleaning

Maintain a healthy workplace with our industrial-grade commercial duct cleaning. We service offices, retail spaces, restaurants, medical facilities, and multi-unit buildings. Our team works around your schedule to minimize disruption.

  • Industrial-grade equipment for large systems
  • Flexible scheduling -- evenings and weekends
  • Compliance with OSHA and EPA standards
  • Multi-unit and high-rise capabilities
  • Detailed service reports provided

Dryer Vent Cleaning

Clogged dryer vents are a leading cause of house fires. Our professional dryer vent cleaning removes built-up lint and debris, reducing fire risk and improving your dryer's efficiency. Most homeowners see reduced drying times immediately.

  • Fire hazard prevention
  • Improved dryer efficiency and performance
  • Complete lint and debris removal
  • Vent line inspection included
  • Reduces energy costs

Duct Sanitization & Deodorizing

After cleaning, our EPA-registered antimicrobial treatment eliminates mold spores, bacteria, viruses, and persistent odors. This is especially recommended for homes with allergy sufferers, pets, smokers, or after water damage events.

  • EPA-registered antimicrobial products
  • Eliminates mold, bacteria, and viruses
  • Safe for families and pets
  • Removes persistent odors
  • Recommended after water damage or renovations
