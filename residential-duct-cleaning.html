Our Process

How We Clean Your Air Ducts

At FreshDuct, we follow a precise and thorough procedure to ensure your air duct system is deeply cleaned and sanitized. Our 3-step process uses professional-grade equipment to eliminate dust, debris, and airborne contaminants — enhancing the overall air quality in your home.

Every job includes a complete cleaning of all supply and return air ducts, register and grille cleaning, and before-and-after photo documentation so you can see the difference for yourself.

Step by Step

Our Professional 3-Step Cleaning Process

1

3-Stage HEPA Filtration Vacuum

Our process begins by securely connecting our state-of-the-art, 3-stage HEPA filtration vacuum system to the main duct line. This powerful vacuum ensures the effective capture of dust, debris, and airborne contaminants as we clean — nothing escapes back into your home.

2

Sealed Negative Air Pressure Cleaning

Once all vents are sealed to optimize suction, we place your entire ventilation system under negative air pressure. This allows us to effectively pull dust and debris through the system. We meticulously clean each duct individually, scrubbing and pushing all dirt and particles down toward the main line, where it is collected, vacuumed, and filtered through our HEPA system.

3

Comprehensive Sanitization

After the cleaning is complete, we sanitize all air passages to eliminate any remaining microorganisms — including bacteria, viruses, pollen, allergens, and pet dander. We use Envirocon, a hospital-grade, environmentally safe disinfectant that is safe for both children and pets. This final step guarantees a completely sanitized and healthy air duct system.
