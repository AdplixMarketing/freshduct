About FreshDuct

Dedicated to delivering cleaner, healthier air for homes and businesses since 2010.

Our Story

Built on Trust, Driven by Quality

FreshDuct was founded with a simple mission: to help families and businesses breathe cleaner, healthier air. What started as a small two-person operation has grown into one of the most trusted air duct cleaning companies in the region.

Over the past 15+ years, we've cleaned over 5,000 homes and commercial properties, earning a reputation for thoroughness, transparency, and outstanding customer service. Every job we take on receives the same level of care and attention to detail.

Our NADCA-certified technicians use the latest equipment and follow strict industry standards to ensure your air ducts are cleaned properly -- not just superficially, but thoroughly from end to end.
