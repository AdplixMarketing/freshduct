Your trusted partner for air duct and vent cleaning. Clean air is essential for a quality life — let us make your space fresh and safe.

Our Story

Built on Honesty, Driven by Quality

Our mission is to ensure that your home or business has clean and healthy air. We specialize in professional cleaning of air ducts, vents, and ventilation systems, helping you improve air quality and reduce the risk of allergies, dust, and bacteria.

Starting as an employee at a 5-star air duct cleaning company opened my eyes. I realized I could take what worked, improve it, and create an even better experience for customers. That's how FreshDuct was born in 2023.

Since then, we've cleaned over 1,500 homes and commercial properties in the greater Rockford area. Using advanced techniques and state-of-the-art equipment, we ensure a thorough cleaning of your ventilation systems — not just superficially, but from end to end. Whether you need regular maintenance or specialized cleaning, we're here to provide fast, efficient, and reliable service.
