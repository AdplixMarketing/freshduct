Air Duct Cleaning FAQ

How often should I have my air ducts cleaned?
+
The National Air Duct Cleaners Association (NADCA) recommends having your air ducts cleaned every 3-5 years. However, you may need more frequent cleaning if you have pets, allergies, smokers in the home, or have recently completed renovations. We offer free inspections to help determine if your ducts need cleaning.
How long does air duct cleaning take?
+
A typical residential duct cleaning takes 3-5 hours depending on the size of your home, number of vents, and the condition of your ductwork. Commercial jobs may take longer depending on the facility size. We'll provide a time estimate during your initial consultation.
Will duct cleaning help with my allergies?
+
Yes! Air duct cleaning removes dust, pollen, pet dander, mold spores, and other allergens that circulate through your HVAC system. Many of our customers report significant improvement in allergy symptoms after cleaning. For maximum benefit, we recommend adding our sanitization treatment.
How much does air duct cleaning cost?
+
Our residential services start at $199 for basic cleaning (up to 10 vents) and $349 for our complete cleaning package which includes sanitization and dryer vent cleaning. Commercial pricing is customized based on your facility. We always provide detailed, transparent quotes with no hidden fees or surprise charges.
What equipment do you use?
+
We use professional-grade HEPA-filtered negative air machines, rotary brush systems, and compressed air tools. Our equipment meets or exceeds NADCA standards. We also use video inspection cameras to show you the condition of your ducts before and after cleaning.
Is air duct cleaning messy?
+
Not at all! Our process uses negative pressure to contain all dust and debris within our equipment. We also use drop cloths and shoe covers to protect your floors and furniture. Your home will actually be cleaner after we leave than before we arrived.
Do you clean the HVAC unit itself?
+
Our Complete Clean package includes cleaning of accessible HVAC components including the blower motor, evaporator coil (if accessible), and the area around the air handler. For deep HVAC unit cleaning or repairs, we can coordinate with trusted HVAC technicians in our network.
Are you licensed and insured?
+
Absolutely. FreshDuct is fully licensed, bonded, and insured. Our technicians are NADCA-certified and undergo continuous training. We carry comprehensive general liability and workers' compensation insurance for your complete peace of mind.
Can dirty ducts affect my energy bills?
+
Yes. When dust and debris build up in your ductwork, your HVAC system has to work harder to push air through, increasing energy consumption. The U.S. Department of Energy estimates that 25-40% of energy used for heating or cooling is wasted due to contaminants in the system. Clean ducts help your system run more efficiently.
Do you offer any guarantees?
+
We offer a 100% satisfaction guarantee on every job. If you're not completely satisfied with our work, we'll come back and re-clean at no additional charge. We also provide before-and-after photo documentation so you can see the results for yourself.
